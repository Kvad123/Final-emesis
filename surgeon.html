TThe Two-faced Medic is a minor antagonist in the 2023 YouTube psychological horror SFM movie, Emesis Blue. He was a RED Medic until he was resurrected as an undead.

He was voiced by Chad Payne

Biography

The Two-Faced Medic was the Medic who brought back Stalingrad from Death but was killed himself. Later he tried to kill Fritz Ludwig but Fritz Stunned him with a broken medigun. However Stalingrad broke into the room that Fritz was in and picked up the Two-faced Medic and crushed his skull.

Appearance

He has a RED Medic's outfit and as his name suggests, he has Two-faces. there is stitching in the middle of his face.

Personality

Before his Resurrection, he seemed like a normal Medic who was interested in playing god. After he respawns he acts more like a zombie, like The Jaunts .
