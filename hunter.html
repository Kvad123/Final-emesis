The Hunter

See you on the other side.

The Hunter is a major antagonist in the 2023 YouTube psychological horror SFM movie, Emesis Blue. He was originally a RED Sniper until he was revived as an undead.

He was voiced by Chad Payne

Biography

He is first seen trying to kill Soldier and the Detective. After Soldier pretends to die from being shot by him he grabs his rocket launcher and fires it at The Hunter and as he tries to run away the explosion blows off his right leg. Before he bleeds to death and comes back as The Hunter he takes the bullets out of the Detective's revolver so his friend the Butcher can take as a captive, even laughing before his first demise. He is later seen back from the dead with a long metal pole in place of his missing limb trying to hunt down and kill the Soldier first using a Sniper rifle and then later using a rocket launcher. Before he can kill Soldier he sees what he thinks is the Butcher only to see it's only the Butcher's head as Fritz Ludwig mutilates him with the axe he used to behead the Butcher, Before the Soldier kills him.
